# NômKey Privacy Policy

**Last updated: July 17, 2026**

NômKey is a Chữ Nôm keyboard and reader. Your privacy matters, and the app is built to keep what you type private.

## What NômKey does with your typing

Everything you type is processed **on your device**. NômKey does not collect, store, log, transmit, or share the text you type. It never sends your keystrokes or messages to us or to anyone else.

## No account, no personal data

NômKey does not require an account. It does not ask for, collect, or store any personal information such as your name, email, contacts, or location.

## Network use

NômKey works fully offline for typing. The only time it uses the network is to download images of rare Chữ Nôm characters that your device's own fonts cannot display, from the public GlyphWiki service (glyphwiki.org). These requests contain only the Unicode code point of the character to be shown. They never include your typed text, messages, or any personal data.

## No analytics, ads, or tracking

NômKey contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third party tracking of any kind.

## Children

NômKey does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children.

## Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this page with a new date.
